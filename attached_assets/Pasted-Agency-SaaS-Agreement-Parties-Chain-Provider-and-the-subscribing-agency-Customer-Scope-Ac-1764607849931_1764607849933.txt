 Agency Terms of Service + Pricing & Messaging Addendum
Purpose. This Addendum supplements the Agency SaaS Agreement (Section 2) and governs pricing, billing, messaging compliance, and approval requirements for agencies using Chain. Capitalized terms have the meanings in the Agreement.
A. Onboarding & First-Month Charges
Plan Selection & Upfront Payment. Upon selecting a plan, Agency will pay: (a) the first monthly Service Fee for the chosen tier in advance, plus (b) a one‑time $100 startup fee (non‑refundable once onboarding begins). Access may be suspended until payment clears.


What “Startup” Covers. Account provisioning, role/permissions setup, sender identity verification (email), initial messaging template review, and registration on A2P 10DLC.


B. SMS Program Requirements (Consent & Approval)
Consent Form. Agency must collect, store, and produce proof of consent for each recipient upon request (including opt‑in text, timestamp, number, source, and opt‑outs).


Brand & Campaign Approval. Service is subject to Agency’s messaging brand and campaign approval by the carrier ecosystem (e.g., A2P 10DLC brand/campaign). Until approved, throughput/deliverability may be limited or blocked.


Registration Costs. Any brand/campaign fees, vetting fees, monthly phone‑number rental (10DLC/TFN), and carrier/provider per‑message fees are pass‑through to Agency. Agency must keep registration current. Agency must keep registration current.


Template Compliance. Agency is responsible for the content it sends (truthful, non‑deceptive, compliant with FDCPA/Reg F, TCPA,STATE LAWS, ETC.). Chain may require edits or suspend templates that present elevated risk.

D. Automatic Tier Adjustments
Auto‑Upgrade. If Agency exceeds included email or SMS volume by >20% in two consecutive months, Agency will automatically move to the next tier effective the next billing cycle.


Downgrade. Agency may request a downgrade once usage remains below the lower tier’s limits for two consecutive months; downgrades take effect the following billing cycle.


E. Billing, Invoices & Taxes
Billing Cycle. Monthly in advance for the Service Fee; overages and pass‑through costs billed in arrears.


Invoices. Due net 15 days unless otherwise stated. Late balances may accrue interest at 1.5%/mo (or the maximum permitted by law) and may trigger suspension.


Taxes. Fees are exclusive of taxes; Agency is responsible for applicable taxes.


F. Messaging Deliverability & Throughput
No Guarantee. Delivery and throughput depend on third‑party networks and brand/campaign ratings; Chain cannot guarantee delivery speed or inbox placement.


Spam/Abuse Controls. Chain and providers may throttle or block traffic that appears abusive, unlawful, or harmful to deliverability.


G. Compliance & Indemnity (Messaging)
Compliance. Agency will comply with FDCPA/Reg F, TCPA, state telemarketing/messaging laws, carrier policies, and any applicable email anti‑spam laws (e.g., CAN‑SPAM).


Audit & Logs. On request, Agency will produce consent logs and campaign records. Failure may result in suspension.


Indemnity. Agency will indemnify Chain for claims, fines, or losses arising from Agency’s messaging content, lack of valid consent, or unlawful contact practices.


H. Suspension; Termination
Cause. Chain may suspend messaging or account access for non‑payment, excessive spam/complaints, failed vetting, or legal/operational risk.


Termination. For persistent breach uncured within 15 days after notice. Upon termination, Agency may export its data for 30 days; continued retention may be required by law.


I. Price Changes
Chain may update fees or overage rates with 30 days’ notice (email or in‑app). Updated fees apply at the next renewal unless Agency terminates before renewal.
J. Order of Precedence
If there is a conflict between this Addendum and the Agreement, this Addendum controls for pricing, messaging, and approval matters.
